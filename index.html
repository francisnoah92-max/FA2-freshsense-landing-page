Why Choose FreshSense?

Mushrooms are sensitive; small environmental changes can ruin an entire harvest. FreshSense provides a "head start" by detecting early smells invisible to humans.

  • Prevents crop loss of 30-70% per harvest.

  • Easy setup: just place it in the grow room and turn it on.
